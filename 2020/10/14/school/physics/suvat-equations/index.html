“suvat” equations

  • equations applied to uniform motion.

  • $s:=s(t)$: displacement (The Latin word for displacement is spatium).

  • $u=v(0)$: initial velocity.
  • $v:=v(t)$: final velocity.
  • $a$: acceleration (“$g$” for gravitational acceleration).
  • $t$: time elapsed.
