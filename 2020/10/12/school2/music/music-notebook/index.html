Areas of Inquiry (AoI)

  1. Music for social-cultural and political expression
  2. Music for listening and performance
  3. Music for dramatic impact, movement and entertainment
  4. Music technology in the electronic and digital age

How to create a music journal

  • Key pieces/ performer/ composer in the same genre or era
  • Music theory
  • AoI - your own perspective
  • Extra-musical findings
  • Sketch creator/ performer ideas

Exploring unfamiliar music

  • Find key composers and performers in genre or related to a piece.
  • Music theory - compositional or performance techniques.
  • AoI - what perspective?
  • Sketch experiment ideas - performer or creator?
  • Extra-musical findings to contextualize
  • Anything else that can influence decision making
  • Include musical examples to support your findings

Absolute and program music

absolute music

  • without lyrics
  • experimental
  • no agenda
  • music for its own sake

    program music

  • specific ideas, stories
  • narrative
