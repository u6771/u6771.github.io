Coordinate systems

n-dimensional Cartesian coordinate system: $\mathbb{R}^n$
n-dimensional Polar coordinate system: $\mathbb{R} \times (\mathbb{R}/2\pi\mathbb{Z})^{n-1}$

Vector notations

  • $\vec{v}_x$: the x component of $\vec{v}$.
  • $\vec{v}_y$: the y component of $\vec{v}$.
  • $|\vec{v}|$: the magnitude of $\vec{v}$.
  • $\vec{v}_\theta$: the angle of $\vec{v}$ from the x-axis.
