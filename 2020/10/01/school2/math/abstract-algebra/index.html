Cartesian product

A Cartesian product of sets $X$ and $Y$ is the set of all ordered pairs $(x,y)$ for $x$ is in $X$ and $y$ is in $Y$.

Algebraic structure

  • An algebraic structure is a set which satisfies a certain properties under some operations.

Group-like (类似群的结构)


Two paths a magma can become a group. We will only discuss the path on the right.
* Both divisibility and invertibility means the existence of inverse elements.

Magma (原群)

  • a magma is a set $G$ that is closed under a binary operation.
    totality: exists $ab \in G$ for all $a,b \in G$.
    * For generality the operator symbol is omitted. We should refer to $ab$ as the product of $a$ and $b$.

Semigroup (半群)

  • a semigroup is a magma with the associative property.
    associativity: $(ab)c = a(bc)$ for all $a,b,c \in G$.

Monoid (幺半群)

  • a monoid is a semigroup with an identity element.
    identity: exists $e \in G$ such that $ea = ae = a$ for all $a \in G$.

Group (群)

  • a group is a monoid with inverse elements.
    invertibility: exists $a^{-1} \in G$ such that $a^{-1}a = aa^{-1} = e$ for all $a \in G$.
     
  • Any group-like structure is said to be commutative if the commutative property is satisfied.
    commutativity: $ab = ba$ for all $a,b \in G$.
  • The order of a group $G$ is the cardinal number $|G|$.
  • $G$ is said to be finite if $|G|$ is finite.

Ring-like (类似环的结构)

Pseudo-ring (伪环)

  • a pseudo-ring (rng) is
    a commutative group under $+$ and
    a semigroup under $\times$ with the distributive property.
    distributivity:
    $a \times (b + c) = a \times b + a \times c$ and
    $(a + b) \times c = a \times c + b \times c$
    for all $a,b,c \in R$
    * the operators $+$ and $\times$ can be defined in whatever ways as long as the properties above holds.

    Ring (环)

  • a ring is a pseudo-ring being a monoid under $\times$.

Commutative ring (交换环)

  • a commutative ring is a ring being a commutative monoid under $\times$.

Division ring (除环)

  • a division ring is a ring being a group under $\times$.

Field-like (类似域的结构)

Field (域)

  • a field is a ring being a commutative group under $\times$.
