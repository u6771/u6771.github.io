Basis

$v_1,\cdots,v_n$ is basis of $V$ if

  • right dimension ($\dim V = n$)
  • spans $V$
  • is linearly independent

Isomorphism

$V$ and $U$ are isomorphic vector spaces if

  • there exists an invertable linear map $T: V \to U$
  • there exists an linear map $T: V \to U$ that is both injective and surjective
  • $\dim V = \dim U$
