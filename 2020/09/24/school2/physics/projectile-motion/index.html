Notations

  • $s_x:=s_x(t)$: horizontal displacement after some time $t$.
  • $s_y:=s_y(t)$: vertical displacement after some time $t$.
  • $u_x=v_x(0)$: initial horizontal velocity.
  • $u_y=v_y(0)$: initial vertical velocity.
  • $v_x:=v_x(t)$: horizontal velocity after some time $t$.
  • $v_y:=v_y(t)$: vertical velocity after some time $t$.
  • $\theta$: initial launch angle.
  • $t_h$: time to reach maximum height.
  • $h$: maximum height.
  • $r$: range.
  • $g$: gravitational acceleration $\approx9.81\,\text{ms}^{-2}$
